FAQ

Questions, answered

Does ListWise AI work with my Shopify theme?

Yes. ListWise AI writes directly to your product title, description, tags, and SEO fields through the standard Shopify Admin API, so it works with any theme.

Will the AI overwrite my listings automatically?

No. You always review a before/after comparison and choose to apply, edit, or discard each suggestion before anything changes in your store.

What happens if I go over my monthly generation limit?

You'll get a friendly notice in the app and can upgrade instantly to a higher plan — no listings are ever lost or blocked.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Billing is handled securely through Shopify, and you can cancel or change plans anytime from your Shopify admin.
