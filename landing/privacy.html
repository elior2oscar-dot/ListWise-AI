Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information ListWise AI ("the App", "we", "us") collects when a merchant installs and uses the App on their Shopify store, and how that information is used.

1. Information we collect

  • Store information: your Shopify store domain (e.g. your-store.myshopify.com) and the OAuth access token issued by Shopify when you install the App, used solely to make authorized API calls on your behalf.
  • Product content: when you choose to generate content for a product, the App sends that product's existing title, description, product type, and vendor to OpenAI's API in order to generate a new title, description, meta description, and tags. We do not send your entire catalog unless you explicitly select multiple products for bulk generation.
  • Usage data: the number of AI generations performed per store per billing period, and a log of which products were generated and applied, used to enforce plan limits and show you your usage dashboard.
  • Brand voice preference: the tone setting (e.g. playful, luxury, minimal) you choose in Settings.

2. How we use this information

We use the information above solely to operate the App's core functionality: authenticating with your store, generating AI content on request, enforcing subscription plan limits, and displaying usage statistics to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Third-party processors

  • OpenAI processes the product content you submit for generation, subject to OpenAI's API data usage policies. As of this writing, OpenAI does not use API-submitted data to train its models by default.
  • Shopify processes billing for paid subscription plans directly; we never see or store your payment card details.
  • Render.com hosts our application server and database.

4. Data retention

We retain session and usage data for as long as the App remains installed on your store, plus a reasonable period afterward for support and legal purposes. Uninstalling the App triggers deletion of your store's session record. You may request full deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 6).

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time. Uninstalling the App from your Shopify admin stops all further data collection immediately.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
