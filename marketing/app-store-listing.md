## App name
ListWise AI — AI Product Descriptions & SEO

## Tagline (under 100 characters)
Write SEO-optimized product titles & descriptions with AI, in one click.

## App introduction (under 100 words)
ListWise AI turns rough product notes into polished, SEO-ready titles, descriptions, meta text, and tags — for one product or your entire catalog. Stop staring at a blank description box. Pick your products, generate on-brand copy in seconds, review a clear before/after comparison, and publish straight to your store. Built for solo sellers and growing brands who want to launch products faster and rank higher in search, without hiring a copywriter.

## Feature list (bullets, for the listing page)
- Generate SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, meta text, and tags with one click
- Bulk-generate content for your entire product catalog at once
- Set a brand voice (playful, luxury, minimal, bold) applied consistently across listings
- Side-by-side before/after review before anything is published
- One-click publish directly to your Shopify product pages
- Usage dashboard to track monthly AI generations against your plan
- No design or writing skills required
